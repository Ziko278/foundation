VINCENT AND ANNE FOUNDATION is a non-governmental, core philanthropic and advocacy organization. The price of the less privileged stemmed our decision to come together and organize a body that will constantly and concertedly proffer assistance to those that are in dire living condition.

Our focus is on protecting and providing for the helpless, the needy, the disadvantaged, and the displaced in the society. We try to revive the hope of the hopeless by giving to their needs. we also speak on their behalf, advocating help for them.

With the aid of nodern technology, we see the world as a global unit that can be toured in a very short time, so we strive to have our presence felt everywhere.

Our Story

We saw the need for energetic, non-profit work within our local community and beyond. After learning about problems frequently faced by families & individuals in the circumstance of conflicts and natural disasters, resulting to displacement and total hardship. We were moved to form a non-profit organization to provide sensible solutions

Learn More about What We Do And Get Involved

Vincent and Anne Foundation convene and collaborate as a community/organization: we bring together philanthropic professionals, public spirited individuals and organization in a view to assisting humanity and also allow for philanthropic professionals play their roles. We design programs that allow people we meet cultivate the habit of sharing love. We advocate for the less fortunate

- We engage in a wide assortment of activities that together support our joint mission
- We are committed to the power of fellowship and collaboration
- we run programs, provide services and conduct direct charitable activities, like providing aid to individual and families for disaster relief and hardship assistance

Our Ideologies

Our Mission

We develop, promote and encourage people participation in charitable work. We build support by acting both on the problem and succour in a digital way, leveraging on this internet and network age.

Our Vision

To see a society where the care and love for humanity is paramount and see help reaching the helpless in time, anywhere they are.

Our Focus

We focus on the HELPLESS.

  • We assist the displaced
  • We care for the sick
  • We give the voiceless a voice, they speak through us.
  • We provide the basic of life where there is much need.
