Financial Assistance for Terkimbir Benjamin

A 46 year old bedridden man from Benue state Terkimbir Benjamin, weds The groom Terkimbir Benjamin Tyough an electrician from Benue state Nigeria. Has been unable to afford the bill for his treatment after falling off an electric pole in march last year injuring his spinal cord. He was rushed to the hospital following the accident but has since been taken home due to his inability to pay the bill of N1.6 million demanded by the hospital management for his surgery. His unfortunate condition did not deter him from tieing the nuptial nut with the mother of his 3 sons they got wedded by Priest Kenneth Agede who described that wedding as a true demonstration of love, appealed to people to show pity and assist the groom get back on his feet by donating for the cost of his surgery so he walk again and enjoy his marriage.
